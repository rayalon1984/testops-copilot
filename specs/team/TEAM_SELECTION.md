# Team Routing Rubric for Persona Selection

## Purpose
This document serves as a guideline for selecting team members based on various personas to ensure optimal team dynamics and project success.

## Personas
1. **The Innovator**  
   - Characteristics: Creative, risk-taker, and visionary.
   - Ideal Roles: Lead Developer, Project Manager.

2. **The Analyst**  
   - Characteristics: Detail-oriented, logical, and systematic thinker.
   - Ideal Roles: Data Analyst, QA Specialist.

3. **The Communicator**  
   - Characteristics: Excellent interpersonal skills, collaboration-focused.
   - Ideal Roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master.

4. **The Supporter**  
   - Characteristics: Empathetic, patient, and reliable.
   - Ideal Roles: Customer Support, Operations.

## Selection Criteria
- Assess each team member against these personas to ensure balanced team composition.
- Prioritize diverse skill sets to cover various aspects of the project requirements.

## Conclusion
Utilizing this rubric will help in forming well-rounded teams that can tackle projects efficiently while leveraging the strengths of each persona.
